THE CHURCH

We love music!

Breaking Forth, Transforming Generations. The Perez Chapel International has a Pentecostal and Charismatic orientation and believes in the totality of the Bible. The Perez Chapel International, formerly known as the Word Miracle Church International started in Tamale, Northern Ghana on 29th March 1987.“The Church has its headquarters in Dzorwulu, Accra. The Church currently has 530 branches in 22 countries. The Perez Dome a 14,000 seater auditorium regarded as the largest auditorium in Ghana, is the leading branch of P.C.I. The senior pastors of the Dome are Bishop Charles and Rev Mrs Vivian Agyinasare.
